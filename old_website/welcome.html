Who we are:

The Harvard Latter-day Saint Student Association is an official student organization formed to promote spiritual well-being, foster and strengthen friendships, and uphold Church standards by sponsoring activities and events for the LDS undergraduates at Harvard. It collaborates with the Cambridge University Ward and the Boston Institute of Religion. The Harvard LDSSA continually seeks to identify and meet the needs of its current and future members.
