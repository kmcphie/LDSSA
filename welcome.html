Harvard College Latter-day Saints Student Association

Welcome

The Harvard College Latter-day Saint Student Association is an official student organization formed to promote spiritual well-being, foster and strengthen friendships, and uphold Church standards by sponsoring activities and events for the LDS undergraduates at Harvard. The Harvard College LDSSA continually seeks to identify and meet the needs of its current and future members.
